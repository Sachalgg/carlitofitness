Our Story

TRAIN
HARDER.
LIVE
BETTER.

Born in the heart of Brussels's European Quarter, Carlito Sport is more than a gym — it's a community built on sweat, ambition, and mutual support. Whether you're taking your first step or crushing personal records, there's a place for you here.

With both indoor facilities at Avenue de Tervueren and outdoor sessions at the iconic Parc du Cinquantenaire athletics track, we bring training to life in every environment.
