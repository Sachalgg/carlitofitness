FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

Can't find your answer? Contact us directly — we're happy to help.

Is the first class really free? +
Yes, completely free. No credit card, no catch. Just book your spot via our free trial page, show up, and experience a full class.
Do I need to be fit to start? +
Not at all. All our group classes are open to all fitness levels. Our coaches adapt exercises to where you are right now — beginners are always welcome.
How do I book ongoing classes? +
All ongoing sessions are booked through our BSport platform. You can view the full schedule, pick your time slot, and pay online. No in-person sign-up required.
Are there long-term contracts? +
No long-term lock-ins. We offer session passes and monthly options with flexibility built in. You can stop, pause, or change your plan at any time.
What about corporate pricing (FIT@Work)? +
Corporate packages are custom-quoted based on team size, frequency, and location preferences. Contact us directly and we'll put together a proposal within 24 hours.
Where are the classes held? +
Indoor classes are held at Avenue de Tervueren 32–34, Etterbeek. Outdoor sessions take place at the Parc du Cinquantenaire athletics track — one of Brussels's most iconic locations.
